# Codebuff for Busy Founders

[Benjamin Bryant](https://x.com/bhbryant) runs an early-stage investment fund that focuses on Y Combinator startups. A lifelong programmer who's been coding since he was "yea tall," Ben built most of his fund's internal technology stack.

He jumps between coding, investor meetings, and founder pitches, so features used to take an afternoon or more.

Codebuff cut most changes to minutes.

> In 10 minutes, Codebuff will get me most of the way there... It's like having an extension of your brain.

He used it to ship a tear-sheet system for portfolio companies. Codebuff scaffolded the PDF generation, API calls, and data models; he tightened imports and structure afterward. It kept his existing patterns instead of dropping in boilerplate.

When you're context-switching all day, that speed matters.
